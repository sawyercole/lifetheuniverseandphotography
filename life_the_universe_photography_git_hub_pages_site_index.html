About

I’m Sawyer Cole, an optics and astronomy grad student who likes cameras almost as much as telescopes. I shoot three main threads: standard photography (people and places), infrared (the desert reimagined), and astrophotography (Milky Way to nebulae). I currently live in Tucson, Arizona, and spend a lot of nights under dark skies.

  • Canon EOS RP / ZWO & ASIAIR rigs
  • IR conversions (590–850 nm)
  • PixInsight • Siril • Photoshop
  • Wyant College of Optical Sciences
